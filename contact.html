FAQs

Quick answers to common questions

How can I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased through our Tickets page which will redirect you to our official ticketing partner. All sales are final.

What is your refund policy?

All ticket sales are final. However, if an event is cancelled or postponed, we will offer refunds or the option to transfer your ticket to the new date.

Are your events accessible?

Yes, all our venues are wheelchair accessible and we provide accommodations for guests with disabilities. Please contact us in advance for specific needs.

Can I volunteer at your events?

Absolutely! We love working with passionate volunteers. Check out our Join Us page for current opportunities.
